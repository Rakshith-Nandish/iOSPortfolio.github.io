Open Bank

An end to end solution for all banking needs for Santander customers

Project summary

The one stop solution for all banking needs of Santander customers. The application lets users manage their accounts in a simple and intuitive way. Users can obtain new cards, obtain loans from the bank, transfer money to other accounts and much more, all of this with the utmost security The application, opens to users a world of investment opportunities, from purchasing stocks, ETFs to trading in F&O. Users can create customized portfolios and track their investments through a variety of charts and fine grain insights.

My Contribution

  • Part of the core team that developed the architecture of the application.
  • Keeping scalability in mind, we decided to have a modular structure for the application. We developed a hybrid MVP-VIPER architecture, while following clean code principles.
  • Followed a test driven approach to development, ensuring that unit tests and UI tests were in place. Git hooks were in place to ensure all tests passed before merging to higher branches. Bitrise was used for CI/CD requirements.
  • Worked extensively on the Wealth vertical of the application, helped gain a good undrestanding of Fintech. The vertical was responsible for handling user portfolios, trading stocks and F&O and much more!.
  • Lead the team responsible for resolving production issues. This involved constant monitoring of the app for crashes and issues, prioritising and delegating the issues.
  • Collaborated with the team leads and developers of other verticals to understand the issue and worked in pairs to solve the issue.
  • Ideated a widget for the application, dedicated to display information of spending on select cards, thus saving the user from having to log into the application and navigate to the cards spending section.
