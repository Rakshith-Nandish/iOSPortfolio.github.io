Lungy

Lungy is a revolutionary breathing application that transforms your breathing experience into an immersive audiovisual experience.

Project summary

Lungy has been developed under the close supervision of Doctors. It is a unique breathing app that recognises and responds to the user's breathing. It provides a one of a kind immersive experience, you can watch beautiful visuals of dandelions dispersing, swirling nebulas and much much more.

My Contribution

  • Handled responsibilities from development to deployment of the application
  • Used the MVVM architecture along with clean swift concepts.
  • Developed the app in UIKit and SwiftUI.
  • Created documentation for the architecture and best practices to be followed, to help other developers
  • Worked on fetching volume data from microphone and displaying graphs thet helped user analyse their breathing
